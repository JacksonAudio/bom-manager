Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Jackson Audio, LLC ("Jackson Audio," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the Jackson Audio BOM Manager application (the "Service"), hosted at bom.jacksonaudio.net.

1. Who We Are

Jackson Audio, LLC is a manufacturer of guitar effects pedals and audio equipment based in Fort Worth, Texas. The BOM Manager is an internal business tool used by Jackson Audio and its authorized brand Fulltone USA to manage bills of materials, production, inventory, and related operations.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the Service:

  • Account Information: Name and email address of authorized team members who log in to the Service.
  • Team Member Contact Information: Phone numbers and email addresses of employees and contractors, provided voluntarily by authorized administrators, for the sole purpose of sending work-related notifications (e.g., build order assignments, production alerts).
  • Product and Inventory Data: Product names, part numbers, serial numbers, pricing, and supplier information entered by authorized users in the course of business operations.
  • Customer Registration Data: When end customers voluntarily register a product (via QR code), we collect their name, email, and the product serial number for warranty and product support purposes.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Internal Operations: Managing production workflows, inventory tracking, build orders, and quality assurance.
  • Transactional Notifications: Sending SMS text messages and emails to our own team members regarding build order assignments, production status updates, play test results, and other time-sensitive operational alerts. These messages are strictly work-related and transactional in nature.
  • Product Registration: Processing voluntary product registrations from customers for warranty support and, with consent, adding them to our email newsletter.

4. SMS/Text Messaging

We send SMS text messages exclusively to Jackson Audio team members (employees and contractors) who have been added to the system by an authorized administrator. Messages are limited to:

  • Build order assignment notifications
  • Build completion confirmations
  • Play test failure alerts requiring immediate attention

These are transactional, non-marketing messages sent only to individuals who are part of our internal team. We do not send promotional or marketing messages via SMS. We do not sell, share, or provide phone numbers to any third parties. Message frequency depends on production activity and is typically fewer than 10 messages per team member per week.

Team members can request removal of their phone number from the system at any time by contacting their administrator or emailing brad@jacksonaudio.net.

Standard message and data rates may apply based on the recipient's mobile carrier plan.

5. Email Communications

We send email notifications to team members for operational alerts (low stock, build completion, play test failures). These are transactional emails, not marketing communications. Customer registration emails are processed through Klaviyo with the customer's consent.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We share data only with the following service providers, solely to operate the Service:

  • Supabase: Database hosting and authentication
  • Vercel: Application hosting
  • Twilio: SMS message delivery to team members
  • Resend: Transactional email delivery
  • Klaviyo: Customer newsletter management (with customer consent only)
  • Shopify: E-commerce order and product data synchronization

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information, including:

  • Row-level security (RLS) on all database tables
  • Authenticated access requiring login credentials
  • Role-based permissions (Admin, team member)
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • API keys stored as environment variables, never in client-side code

8. Data Retention

We retain team member contact information only for as long as the individual is an active member of our team. Upon departure, administrators can remove their information from the system. Product and inventory data is retained indefinitely for business record-keeping purposes.

9. Your Rights

Team members may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal information by contacting their administrator or emailing brad@jacksonaudio.net.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is a business tool not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
